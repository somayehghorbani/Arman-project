About US

Home >> About US

ARMAN INSULATORS (Maghareh Sazan Arman - MSA) Is One Of The Most Reliable Manufacturers Of Composite Insulators In West Asia. ARMAN Group Established In 2003, and Started To Produce Various Types Of Composite Insulators Up To 400 KV Level From 2013. In The Past Almost One Decade, We Supplied Both The Local Market And Also Start Exporting To The Neighbor Countries.

The Quality Is Our Commitment. ARMAN INSULATORS Has Built A Strong Team Of Highly Skilled And Well-Trained Experts. Moreover, The ARMAN Laboratory Is Equipped And Audited Continuously To Give The Best Services To The Factory, Research And Development Team And The Clients. The Combination Of Modern Equipment And Technologies With The Establishment Of Quality Control Systems In All The Organizational Levels Has Made Us Trusted Partners Of Our Esteemed Clients.

Our Infrastructure Comprises A State Of The Art Manufacturing Plant Located In Paytakht Industrial Zone, in East Of Tehran. Modern Equipment Adapted To Ensure Precision And High Quality. Our Main Asset Is The Team Of Qualified Professionals And Engineers Who Are Geared With The Latest Knowledge And Resources.

Quality Assurance System Covers All The Processes and Operational Activities Such As Design, Raw Material Supply, Production, Quality Control, Sales and After-Sales Services. Moreover, the system is eager for continuous improvement to simplify products and processes. Commitment To Consistent Quality And Reliability Drive Our Systems.
